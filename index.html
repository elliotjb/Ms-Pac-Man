Welcome to Tomatic Gang's page.

Welcome to our web of Ms. Pac-Man, a tribute game who has been made by five students of CITM University, an affiliated center of the UPC, during the first year of the career for the subject Project.

Why Ms. Pac-Man?

Ms. Pac-Man is an arcade video game from the Golden Age, produced by Illinois-based Bally/Midway Manufacturing corporation, the North American publisher of Pac-Man, one of the most popular arcade video games of all time. We choose this game due to her complexity and the challenge to recreate one of our favourites games of our lifes.

What role each member of the group has developed.

Elliot Jimènez has been the manager of the group, ensuring each task are delivered on time, helping all the members during each tasks.

Arnau Morgado has been the QA, reporting all the bugs and helping to code the game.

Albert Llopart i Gerard Gil has been the programming responsible, ensuring the code it's well-made and it's comprehensive.

Alfonso Sánchez has been the artist responsable, searching all the sprites. However, everyone has helped each other in her tasks.
